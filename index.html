PowerSchool SIS

K-12 Student Information System

PowerSchool SIS is a comprehensive system with extensive, configurable features to meet the needs of schools and districts of all sizes and types, including public, charter, private, international schools, and more.

PowerSchool’s strength is flexibility, and because of that flexibility, a school district can align PowerSchool’s presentation of information more closely to its vision for what matters to students. The information they receive from their SIS is presented not in a way that serves some prototype school district, but in a way that has been tailored to their specific school district.

The Benefits of a Secure, Flexible, & Compliant SIS

Configure to Your Specific Needs
Add data fields and pages and extend tables to collect any of the data you need. Use flexible scheduling, attendance, grading, calendaring, and reporting.
Promote Family Engagement
Grant student and family access to grades, attendance, and progress from the mobile app or parent/student portals to improve accountability and communication.
Ensure Data Security
Our security and compliance experts help keep data safe and accessible. Optional cloud hosting with Microsoft Azure improves security and reduces costs and maintenance time.
Maximize School Funding
Accurate compliance reporting ensures maximum school funding. Our compliance experts stay on top of education department requirements and reporting changes, to provide you a library of pre-built reports.

How PowerSchool Protects Data

We are dedicated to protecting your students’ data with a comprehensive security program that starts with “secure by design” principles at the inception of our products and extends through third-party penetration testing, robust cloud security, and a fully staffed 24x7x365 Security Operations Center. Our products are independently validated by third-party auditors, ensuring your data is always protected with PowerSchool. To learn more visit our security page.
